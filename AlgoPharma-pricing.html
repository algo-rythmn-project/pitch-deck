Why AlgoPharma™ pays for itself
Designed for one pharmacist, not a compliance department
Most compliance software is sold by the seat to hospitals and chains. AlgoPharma™ is priced and built for the independent pharmacist who is also the licence holder. $100 / month is the entire cost — not per seat, not plus consulting hours.
Trained on your documents — and on Alberta
Generic AI answers from the public internet. AlgoPharma™ answers from your uploaded SOPs, your inspection history, and the actual ACP regulations — with citations to the exact source. Every answer is traceable to a verifiable document.
Your data stays in Canada
Documents are stored in AWS Canada-region infrastructure, aligned to HIA data residency expectations. Patient information stays inside Canadian borders. You control what's uploaded and what's deleted.
One avoided deficiency covers the year
ACP hearing processes start at $28,640 in legal cost, borne by the pharmacist personally. Re-inspections cost $250–$500 in fees plus days of preparation. $1,000 / year is roughly 0.27% of an average pharmacy's gross profit — and one good response makes the math obvious.
Aware of the new SOLP framework
February 2025 brought a new outcomes-based standards framework. Most independents are still working off pre-2025 SOPs. AlgoPharma™ scans your documents, flags gaps, and drafts compliant updates — so the next inspection isn't the moment you find out you were behind.
Limits, not lockouts
The 150 included credits per month flow freely across Q&A, SOP Intelligence, CQI+, and Inspection Report responses — no per-tool caps. When something unusual happens, top-up credits are there. You're never forced into a more expensive plan to handle one busy month.
Common questions, answered honestly
?$100 a month is a lot for a small pharmacy.
It's about $3 a day. One avoided re-inspection ($250–$500 in fees plus days of preparation) covers months of subscription. One avoided deficiency response error covers years. AlgoPharma™ is priced below the cost of a single hour of pharmacy consulting per month.
?We already have a compliance consultant.
A consultant is invaluable for novel legal questions — AlgoPharma™ doesn't replace that. But a consultant isn't in the room when a PPC walks in unannounced at 9am on a Tuesday. Different problems, different tools. Most pharmacists keep both.
?Our SOPs are already in SharePoint or Google Drive.
SharePoint stores files. AlgoPharma™ answers questions from them — by SOLP standard number, in plain language, with cited sources — in seconds. SharePoint can't tell an inspector which SOP maps to Standard 12.
?We haven't had problems in past inspections.
A clean history is worth protecting. Were your SOPs written before February 2025? The new SOLP framework took effect then. A clean record under the old standards isn't a guarantee under the new ones. Basic ($500) lets you check before committing further.
?Will my data be safe? Where is it stored?
Documents are stored in AWS Canada-region infrastructure, aligned to HIA expectations. Each Professional account has its own isolated namespace. Query audit logs are exportable. You can delete uploaded documents at any time.
?What if I run out of monthly limits?
Most months you won't. When you do, buy a top-up bundle of credits. You don't have to upgrade your plan to handle one busy month. The 50- and 150-credit packs offer a discount for stocking up.
?Can I cancel? Do I get locked in?
Monthly Professional cancels any time, end of billing period. Annual plans run for the term you paid for; we don't pro-rate refunds, but you keep full access until the term ends. No multi-year contracts. No exit fees.
?How is this different from ChatGPT?
ChatGPT answers from the public internet. AlgoPharma™ answers from your documents and ACP-specific data, with cited sources, version-controlled SOPs, and a regulator-aware metadata layer. An inspector can verify every answer.
