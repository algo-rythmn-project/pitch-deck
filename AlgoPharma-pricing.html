Why AlgoPharma™ pays for itself
Designed for one pharmacist, not a compliance department
Most compliance software is sold by the seat to hospitals and chains. AlgoPharma™ is priced and built for the independent pharmacist who is also the licence holder. $100 / month is the entire cost — not per seat, not plus consulting hours.
Answers from your documents — and from Alberta
Generic AI answers from the public internet. AlgoPharma™ answers from your uploaded SOPs, your inspection history, and the actual ACP regulations — with citations to the exact source. Every answer is traceable to a verifiable document.
Your data stays in Canada — fully
Documents are stored in AWS Canada-region infrastructure. AI processing also runs on Canadian infrastructure, so query content does not leave Canadian borders. Aligned to Alberta's Health Information Act (HIA) data residency expectations. You control what's uploaded and what's deleted.
One avoided deficiency covers the year
ACP hearing process costs in recent published decisions have run from roughly $8,000 to $78,000+, with pharmacists ordered to pay 50–100% personally.* Re-inspections cost $250–$500 in fees plus days of preparation. $1,000 / year is a fraction of a single hearing-cost order — and one good response makes the math obvious.
Aware of the new SOLP and SPPPT frameworks
February 2025 brought two new outcomes-based standards: the Standards for the Operation of Licensed Pharmacies (SOLP) and the Standards of Practice for Pharmacists and Pharmacy Technicians (SPPPT). Most independents are still working off pre-2025 SOPs. AlgoPharma™ scans your documents against both frameworks, flags gaps, and drafts compliant updates — so the next inspection isn't the moment you find out you were behind.
Limits, not lockouts
The 150 included credits per month flow freely across Q&A, SOP Intelligence, and Inspection Report responses — no per-tool caps. When something unusual happens, top-up credits are there. You're never forced into a more expensive plan to handle one busy month.
Common questions, answered honestly
?$100 a month is a lot for a small pharmacy.
It's about $3 a day. One avoided re-inspection ($250–$500 in fees plus days of preparation) covers months of subscription. One avoided hearing-cost order — which has run from $8,000 to $78,000+ in recent ACP decisions* — covers years. AlgoPharma™ is priced below the cost of a single hour of pharmacy consulting per month.
?We already have a compliance consultant.
A consultant is invaluable for novel legal questions — AlgoPharma™ doesn't replace that. But a consultant isn't in the room when a PPC walks in unannounced at 9am on a Tuesday. Different problems, different tools. Most pharmacists keep both.
?Our SOPs are already in SharePoint or Google Drive.
SharePoint stores files. AlgoPharma™ answers questions from them — by SOLP standard number, in plain language, with cited sources — in seconds. SharePoint can't tell an inspector which SOP maps to Standard 12.
?We haven't had problems in past inspections.
A clean history is worth protecting. Were your SOPs written before February 2025? The new SOLP and SPPPT frameworks took effect then. A clean record under the old standards isn't a guarantee under the new ones. Basic ($500) lets you check before committing further.
?Will my data be safe? Where is it stored?
Documents are stored in AWS Canada-region infrastructure, and AI processing runs on Canadian infrastructure too — query content does not cross the border. Aligned to HIA expectations. Each Professional account has its own isolated namespace. Query audit logs are exportable. You can delete uploaded documents at any time.
?What if I run out of monthly limits?
Most months you won't. When you do, buy a top-up bundle of credits. You don't have to upgrade your plan to handle one busy month. The 50- and 150-credit packs offer a discount for stocking up.
?Can I cancel? Do I get locked in?
Monthly Professional cancels any time, end of billing period. Annual plans run for the term you paid for; we don't pro-rate refunds, but you keep full access until the term ends. No multi-year contracts. No exit fees.
?How is this different from ChatGPT?
ChatGPT answers from the public internet. AlgoPharma™ answers from your documents and ACP-specific data, with cited sources, version-controlled SOPs, and a regulator-aware metadata layer. An inspector can verify every answer.
Quick answers
What's the difference between monthly and annual on Professional?
Same features, same limits. Annual is $1,000 vs. $1,200 paid monthly — a $200 saving. Most pharmacists on an inspection cycle pick annual.
Can I switch tiers later?
Yes. Upgrade at any time — billed pro-rata. Downgrade at the next renewal.
Do top-up credits expire?
No — top-up credits don't expire while your subscription is active. They roll forward month to month for as long as you're subscribed. If you cancel, any unused top-up credits void at the end of your final billing period. Note: the 50 (Basic) or 150 (Pro) credits included each month reset at the start of each billing cycle — only top-up credits roll over.
Is there a free trial?
Yes — no credit card required on any tier. Trial credits let you run real queries so you can judge the value before committing. Basic: 3-day trial · 10 credits (enough for ~30 AI Q&A questions). Pro Monthly: 3-day trial · 15 credits (Q&A + a couple of SOP scans). Pro Annual: 7-day trial · 25 credits (full feature access with meaningful headroom). Enterprise: 14-day pilot with custom trial credits scoped to your location count.
Do I need to be a tech expert to use this?
No. Upload your SOPs, ask questions in plain language, get cited answers. The interface is built for pharmacists, not developers.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
You can export everything. After 30 days following cancellation, uploaded documents are permanently deleted from the platform.
