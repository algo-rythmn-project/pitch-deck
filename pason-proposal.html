AI Solutions
REAL-TIME
DRILLING PREDICTION ENGINE
Real-time multi-agent AI that predicts well events before they happen — turning EDR telemetry into proactive drilling decisions.
Stuck Pipe & Downhole Anomaly Prediction
Multi-variate models trained on WOB, torque, drag, and MSE patterns predict stuck pipe events 10–20 minutes before occurrence, triggering automated driller alerts and recommended corrective parameters.
Next-Gen Kick Detection & Influx Prediction
Extends Pason's Smart Alarms with predictive influx AI — detecting formation pressure signatures before the pit gain occurs. Integrates mud skid data for real-time formation evaluation.
Bit & Equipment Wear Forecasting
Continuous degradation modeling of PDC bits and downhole tools using ROP efficiency curves and MSE trends — recommending optimal pull times before catastrophic failure.
ROP Optimization Agent
Autonomous drilling parameter recommendations (WOB, RPM, flow rate) benchmarked against best-performing offsets in Pason's basin-wide dataset — continuously updated per formation.

FRAC OPTIMIZATION
INTELLIGENCE
Intelligent multi-agent orchestration for completions workflows — optimizing frac sequencing, valve management, and wellsite safety on IWS infrastructure.
Frac Stage Optimization Agent
AI analyzes real-time pump pressure, slurry rate, and treating pressure data from iECR to dynamically recommend stage design adjustments, fluid blending changes, and perforation cluster targeting in real time.
Valve Sequencing Intelligence
Augments IWS automated valve management with predictive logic — anticipating sequencing bottlenecks, preventing pressure anomalies, and recommending workflow changes before crew intervention is needed.
Wellsite Safety AI
Multi-modal monitoring of completions safety parameters — detecting abnormal pressure builds, equipment stress signatures, and operational deviations that precede well control incidents or equipment failures.
Cross-Stage Learning & Benchmarking
AlgoMind™ agents accumulate learnings across every completed stage and pad — building an operator-specific playbook that continuously improves completion designs from the first stage to the thousandth.
