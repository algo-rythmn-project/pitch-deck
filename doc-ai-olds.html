The thesis

Every regulator in central Alberta already runs an inspection cycle.
Nobody runs an AI Q&A layer over the documents that cycle inspects.

Published corpus. Recurring inspection. Accountable operator. That pattern repeats across Mountain View County's 347 municipal offices, 12,000 Alberta farms with hired labour, and 4,500 confined feeding operations — stress-tested against five criteria.

Compliance buyers don't pay for "AI." They pay for "answer this question against my policy in 6 seconds before the inspector walks in."

That buyer runs a municipality under the Municipal Government Act, a farm under the Farm Freedom & Safety Act (2021), or a confined feeding operation under AOPA. Olds College trains the operators who run these organizations — making it the ideal anchor for reaching this market.

AlgoPharma™ proves the model. Olds College provides the door. Central Alberta is the market.
