The Challenge

Highly manual, document‑heavy,
and disconnected systems.

Multi‑Source Data Retrieval

Pulling data from GeoScout, IRIS, PetroNinja, and 40+ PDFs per well (well tickets, core, DSTs, reports). Everything is spread across disconnected systems, creating friction and long cycle times.

Manual Interpretation

Labor-intensive manual reading of logs (induction, sonic), extracting core porosity/perm, interpreting DST flow periods, and visually assessing production decline and seismic maps.

Cumbersome Prospect Screening

Filtering by Frobisher, Alida, Tilston, and Midale plays, identifying analogs, cross-checking historical production, and ranking prospects relies heavily on manual collation.

70-90% of workflow automated
40+ documents per well ingested instantly
1 unified prospect package generated
02 / 08

The Solution

A Subsurface Intelligence Layer

We propose an AI-driven Subsurface Intelligence Layer that automates the entire workflow from data ingestion to prospect ranking—accelerating prospecting, reducing manual interpretation time, and improving consistency.

1

Automated Data Ingestion

Retrieves and normalizes well logs, core analysis, DSTs, geological reports, production history, and seismic outlines across 40+ documents per well seamlessly.

2

Automated Interpretation

Extracts formation tops, porosity curves, DST flow periods, and core sweet spots directly from PDFs and LAS files, replacing manual log reading.

3

Automated Production & Seismic Mapping

Computes decline curves, identifies analog wells, estimates EUR, and digitizes seismic metadata to uncover coverage gaps and recommend acquisition.

4

Prospect Ranking Engine

Scores prospects utilizing reservoir quality, DST results, production analogs, seismic coverage, and structural position to yield reliable confidence scores.

5

Prospect Package Generation

Outputs complete prospect reports integrating maps, logs, cross-plots, analog matches, risk analysis, and economic indicators—replacing days of tedious compilation.

How it Works

Built for oil & gas data,
not generic enterprise.

Data Layer

GeoScout & IRIS API Connectors — Automated well data ingestion with incremental updates

LAS File Parser — Standardized well log curve extraction (GR, ILD, NPHI, RHOB, DT)

PDF Intelligence — LLM-powered extraction from geological reports, tour reports, and DST summaries

Spatial DB — PostGIS-backed well and seismic location store with DLS/NTS grid support

AI / ML Models

Petrophysical Interpreter — Automated zone picking for Mississippian formations (Alida, Frobisher, Tilston, Midale)

Analog Similarity Engine — Vector embeddings of play characteristics matched against your historical production database

Prospect Ranking Model — Gradient-boosted ensemble combining log, core, production, and spatial features

Explainability Layer — SHAP values on every score so your geologist understands and can override AI recommendations

Interface & Delivery

Interactive Prospect Map — Web-based, GeoScout-like UX with ranked prospects overlaid on DLS grid

One-Click Drill-Down — From map marker to full well profile: logs, core plot, production curve, analog comps

Configurable Filters — Your exclusion criteria (SW Heavy Oil, Bakken, Souris Valley) as toggle controls, adjustable without code changes

Export-Ready — Prospect packages export to PDF for partner presentations or internal review
