Critical Data Gap to Fill

Existing public datasets (SSURGO, etc.) provide soil data but lack the critical connection between soil DNA and crop outcomes. We need to build this proprietary dataset through targeted field trials focusing on:

  1. Extreme ends of the spectrum: Both low and high disease pressure fields
  2. Controlled comparisons: Same fields with different management responses
  3. Longitudinal data: Tracking the same fields over multiple seasons
