10 Data Requirements
What we need from your cooperative

Low lift on your IT team.
High return on ours.

The pilot runs on data you already hold in your ERP and credit systems. A partial export of a 200–500-member subset is enough to get started. Full-base integration comes after the pilot validates results.

Pilot (200–500 members)
  • › Member master — member ID, region, crop type(s), total planted area. Last 3–5 seasons.
  • › Credit & repayment history — loan amounts, due dates, settlement dates, any renegotiations. Anonymised IDs sufficient.
  • › Input purchase records — fertiliser, seed, and crop-protection orders per member, per season.
  • › Grain delivery records — contracted vs. delivered volumes per member, per crop, per season.
Full scope (production SaaS)
  • › All of the above — full member base, 5–10 years of history where available.
  • › Plot boundaries (optional) — GIS shapefiles or cadastral codes. Enables satellite-imagery grounding and meaningfully improves accuracy.
  • › ERP / CRM read access — API or nightly export from Sysagri, AgroSys, or equivalent. No write access required.
  • › Member service interactions (optional) — call logs or agronomy visit records, if digitised. Adds a churn-risk signal layer.
Integration note

We work from flat-file exports (CSV, XLSX, Parquet) or direct API. No co-op has required more than two weeks of IT time for a pilot data transfer. We provide a data-mapping template and handle all transformation on our side.
