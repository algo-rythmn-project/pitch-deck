03 The Problem
A problem hiding in plain sight

Billion-dollar decisions,
made on Excel and tribal knowledge.

The co-op's biggest decisions — how much fertilizer to pre-buy, how to size grain positions, which members to extend credit to — are enterprise-level. But they only get smart when they're grounded in member-level truth. Today, that grounding is missing. The bridge is Excel and tribal knowledge.

The hidden cost
  • › Member default events cluster in bad seasons — exactly when the co-op's balance sheet is most exposed.
  • › Input procurement is locked months ahead on aggregate guesses, not member-level demand signals.
  • › Aggregate trading positions are sized without knowing what 5,000 individual farms will actually deliver.
  • › Member-facing agronomy advice doesn't scale — and rival co-ops compete intensely on exactly this service.

13 Data Requirements
What we need from the cooperative to get started

Minimal lift on your side.
Maximum signal on ours.

The pilot requires two categories of data — a pilot subset for the 90-day engagement, and full-base exports for the production SaaS. All transfers happen inside your own cloud environment.

Pilot (200–500 members)
  • › Member master — member ID, region, crop type(s), total planted area. Last 3–5 seasons.
  • › Credit & repayment history — loan amounts, due dates, settlement dates, any renegotiations. Anonymised member IDs sufficient.
  • › Input purchase records — fertiliser, seed, and crop-protection orders per member, per season.
  • › Grain delivery records — contracted vs. delivered volumes per member, per crop, per season.
Full scope (production SaaS)
  • › All of the above — full member base, 5–10 years of history where available.
  • › Plot boundaries (optional) — GIS shapefiles or cadastral codes. Enables satellite-imagery grounding. Meaningfully improves default-risk accuracy.
  • › ERP / CRM read access — API or nightly export from Sysagri, AgroSys, or equivalent. No write access required.
  • › Member service interactions (optional) — call logs or agronomy visit records, if digitised. Adds a churn-risk signal layer.
Integration note

We work from flat-file exports (CSV, XLSX, Parquet) or direct API. No co-op has required more than two weeks of IT time to complete the initial data transfer for a pilot. We provide a data-mapping template and handle all transformation on our side.
