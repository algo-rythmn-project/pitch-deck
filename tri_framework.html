The Agricultural Intelligence Gap

Today's agriculture generates massive amounts of data but lacks the plant intelligence to turn it into unique and actionable insights. The three critical factors in plant performance remain siloed:

Hybrid Intelligence

Which hybrids perform best where? Limited understanding of genotype × environment interactions.

Environmental Factors

Micro-weather, disease pressure, and stress events are analyzed in isolation, not in combination.

Soil Potential

Soil constraints remain a black box without expensive, time-consuming testing. Only ~40% of farmland is soil tested today.

The Result:

Optimization Gap

Hybrid companies can't understand how genetics perform across real‑world environments.

Targeting Gap

Biological companies don't know which soils or conditions will actually respond to their products.

Actionability Gap

Farmers lack clear, trustworthy insights — and don't know which biologicals to believe.
