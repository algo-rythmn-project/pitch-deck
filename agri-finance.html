Product 01 · Early Yield Signal

We know the yield
before the print lands.

An early yield signal that fuses satellite imagery, live weather, and agentic AI into a harvest estimate weeks ahead of the official number — the hardest test in the chain, and the IP every downstream product stands on.

Pillar 01 · Vegetation

Field-level crop stress, every two weeks

Earth-observation satellites measure how each crop is developing continuously across the season — peak greenness, cumulative vigour, and the precise day the canopy turns. This is the primary predictive signal in every market we cover.

Pillar 02 · Weather & Climate

The conditions that move yield away from expectation

High-resolution daily climate reanalysis delivers temperature, precipitation, growing-degree days, extreme heat events, and soil moisture at field scale — the variables that explain why a crop outperforms or falls short of what the satellite sees.

Pillar 03 · Ground Truth

Official government yield records as the benchmark

Every model is validated against the authoritative national yield series in each market, ensuring the signal is measured against the same number the market ultimately prices — not a proxy.

All data sourced from public, auditable repositories. Crop-specific field masks applied in each market. The signal Products 02 & 03 run on.
