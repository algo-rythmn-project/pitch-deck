01 · Product Overview

What AlgoPharma™ Does

AlgoPharma™ covers the full inspection lifecycle for Alberta pharmacy operators — from preparing before an inspector arrives, to answering questions in real time during a visit, to generating compliant responses and corrective documents after a report is issued.

Target Users

Alberta pharmacy owners, Designated Managers (DMs), and compliance officers responsible for ensuring operations meet Alberta College of Pharmacy (ACP) standards, including the SOLP and SPPPT frameworks.

Core Value Proposition

AlgoPharma™ covers the full ACP inspection lifecycle. Before an inspection: build and maintain compliant SOPs, and use Pre-Inspection Intelligence to know what inspectors focus on. During an inspection: answer any compliance question in seconds using the AI chat, grounded in ACP regulations and your own uploaded documents. After an inspection: upload the report, detect every finding, and generate compliant responses, SOPs, Policies, Checklists, and Log Tables — ready for official submission.

Important Notices
Scope What this platform does not cover

AlgoPharma™ is purpose-built as an operational compliance platform, not a clinical or patient-facing system. It does not handle prescriptions, theranostics, patient records, dispensing, EMR, point-of-sale, or advanced inventory tracking.

Professional Responsibility Human oversight and AI fallibility

AlgoPharma™ augments administrative workflows; it does not replace the professional judgment of a licensed pharmacist. AI-generated documents, chat responses, and regulatory interpretations may contain inaccuracies and must be reviewed and independently verified by a qualified compliance officer before adoption or submission. Accountability for compliance adherence remains with the pharmacy's licence holder and Designated Manager.

03 · Add-on Modules

Purpose-Built Compliance Add-ons

Three additional modules in development, each addressing a distinct regulatory domain. Available as add-ons to any Professional or Enterprise plan; activate only what your pharmacy needs.

Quality Improvement · ACP-Aligned Coming

CQI+ Documentation

Generates the ACP-aligned narrative, root-cause analysis, and SMART action plan as a single document for official submission. The module guides the user from incident logging through regulator-ready package in a structured, fielded workflow.

Federal CDSA Compliance Coming

Controlled Drug Intelligence

A retrieval-first record system for purchases, dispensings, reconciliations, and incidents under the Controlled Drugs and Substances Act (CDSA). Designed for the Pharmacy Manager who carries personal accountability under federal regulation, with the documentation surfaced and queryable on demand.

NAPRA Compliance · CSP Records Coming

Sterile Compounding Intelligence

End-to-end documentation for sterile compounding practice, covering NAPRA-specific metadata and a 24-month inspection calendar. Provides a structured, point-in-time record of each component of the compounding process.
