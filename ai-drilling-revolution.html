Precision Forecasting

Turn historical data into accurate AFE predictions before you spud.

The Problem We Solve

AFEs are often built on outdated offset data, guesswork, and Excel spreadsheets that don't account for local geology variations or recent drilling techniques. This leads to budget overruns of 15-30% on average.

Without accurate forecasting, you're either leaving money on the table with conservative bids or risking capital with optimistic ones.

How It Works

Our system scans thousands of offset wells in your basin, matching geology, depth, and equipment profiles to your planned well.

We use machine learning to identify patterns in historical performance, adjusting for:

  • Formation hardness changes
  • Weather delays by season
  • Equipment failure rates
  • Local crew experience levels

Key Features

  • Real-time adjustment: Updates predictions as actual drilling data comes in
  • Risk-adjusted scenarios: Shows best-case, worst-case, and most likely outcomes
  • Vendor performance tracking: Factors in your specific service company track records
  • Regulation impact modeling: Accounts for local compliance requirements

Expected Outcomes

  • 90%+ accuracy on AFE time/cost predictions
  • 15-25% reduction
  • Bid confidence scoring for competitive situations
  • Daily progress tracking against forecast

To get started, we need access to:

Historical AFEs & Actuals

Previous well budgets vs. actual costs

Geological Data

Formation tops, pore pressure, lithology logs

Rig & Equipment Specs

Rig capabilities, BHA configurations, mud systems

Time & Depth Data

Daily drilling reports with time breakdowns

We can work with whatever format you have - Excel, PDFs, or direct database connections. No data cleanup needed on your end.

24/7 Operations Monitor

A digital watchdog that never sleeps, watching every sensor on your rig.

The Problem We Solve

Engineers can't watch every screen 24/7. By the time a human notices abnormal pressure or torque, the problem may have already escalated to stuck pipe, kick, or equipment failure.

Most monitoring systems only alert when thresholds are crossed - we detect anomalies hours earlier by recognizing patterns that precede failures.

How It Works

We deploy specialized AI "agents" that monitor specific data streams:

  • Torque & Drag Agent: Watches for stuck pipe precursors
  • Pressure Agent: Monitors ECD, SPP, and pit volume
  • Vibration Agent: Detects harmful drill string vibrations
  • Gas Agent: Analyzes gas readings for kicks

These agents collaborate to provide holistic alerts with root cause analysis.

Key Features

  • Predictive alerts: 2-4 hours advance warning of potential issues
  • Root cause analysis: Explains "why" something is happening
  • Actionable recommendations: Suggests specific parameter adjustments
  • Multi-well dashboard: Monitor all active wells from one screen
  • Mobile alerts: Push notifications to phones/tablets

Expected Outcomes

  • 15-20% reduction in non-productive time (NPT)
  • Early detection of 85% of stuck pipe events
  • Reduced equipment damage from vibration/harmonics
  • Fewer safety incidents from kicks and well control events

Data Requirements

Real-time data feeds from rig sensors (WITS/WITSML preferred):

Surface Parameters

Hook load, torque, RPM, ROP, SPP, flow rates

Mud System Data

Pit volumes, mud weight, temperature, rheology

Gas Data

Total gas, C1-C5 components, connection gas

Downhole Data (if available)

MWD/LWD logs, vibration, downhole pressure

We can connect to most rig data systems (WITS, WITSML, OPC-UA) or work with CSV exports. Historical data helps train the system faster.

Institutional Memory

Capture and access decades of drilling experience in seconds.

When experienced personnel retire or move on, their knowledge leaves with them. Lessons learned from previous wells are buried in unsearchable PDFs, emails, and personal notebooks.

Repeating the same mistakes costs millions. Re-discovering solutions that already exist wastes engineering time.

How It Works

Our system ingests all your historical data and documents, then creates a searchable knowledge base:

  • Natural language search: Ask questions in plain English
  • Context-aware retrieval: Finds relevant information based on your current operation
  • Pattern recognition: Identifies recurring problems and solutions
  • Expertise mapping: Knows who in your organization has relevant experience

Key Features
