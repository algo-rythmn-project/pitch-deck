What It Is

A supervisor, and a team of specialists —
not a single chatbot.

AgroMind is a multi-agent reasoning system. A supervisor agent breaks down your question, delegates to specialist agents built for specific tasks, and assembles their findings into one governed report — with the full chain of reasoning attached.
