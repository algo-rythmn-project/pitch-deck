The Problem

Large precision ag operations are data-rich,
but the intelligence layer is missing.

Satellite passes, IoT sensors, weather feeds, equipment telemetry, most of it sits in silos. The data exists. What’s missing is a system that can reason across it and return a cited, auditable answer.

Operations

Data silos, decisions by instinct

Field data, satellite imagery, and equipment logs rarely speak to each other. Decisions get made on incomplete, unreconciled pictures.

Agronomy

No traceable reasoning across data sources

Agronomists pull from multiple systems but can’t produce a cited, auditable answer. Analysis stays locked in individual expertise.

Leadership

Strategic calls without interrogatable evidence

Yield forecasts and resource plans are built on static reports that can’t be queried, updated, or audited in real time.
