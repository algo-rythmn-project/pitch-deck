The Problem

Agri-finance teams handle a lot of data —
much of it contradictory and delayed.

CONAB revises crop numbers between reports. Data arrives from multiple sources on no fixed schedule. We don’t claim deep agri-finance expertise — but we build systems that make contradictory data more manageable.

Cooperative

Many members, inconsistent analysis

Decisions vary across members when there’s no shared, repeatable standard for reading the same data.

Rural Lender

Loan risk priced on forecasts that get revised

Crop forecasts can be revised after a loan is extended — making early signal useful even when imperfect.

Crop Insurer

Slow data, time-sensitive decisions

Regional estimates often arrive after the window to act. Faster reconciliation means less reliance on lagging figures.
