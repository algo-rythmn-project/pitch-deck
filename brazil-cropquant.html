How It Works

Not a single forecast.
A belief that sharpens with every new fact.

CropQuant starts from a structural yield-curve prior, then runs a continuous probabilistic update every time real-world data arrives — narrowing the probability distribution from a wide guess to a sharp, high-confidence call.

P
Day 0–2 · Prior

Structural base signal sets the starting belief

A structural curve prior generates a wide, honestly uncertain initial distribution — no field data yet, no false confidence.

U2
Day 3–10 · Update

Storage, transport, and logistics data tighten the curve

Storage reports, transport logs, and dispatch data feed a probabilistic update — and the distribution visibly narrows.

U5
Day 11–20 · Convergence

Yield surveys and satellite data sharpen the final call

Field yield surveys and weather/satellite reads drive the last updates — belief converges to a tall, narrow, high-confidence peak you can act on.

// The output at every stage is a directional call — WIDEN or NARROW — with an explicit confidence score. Probabilistic updating means the model tells you not just its answer but how sure it is right now, so a trader, lender, or insurer can size the decision proportionally. Built for Brazil’s actual data reality: irregular satellite passes, inconsistent reporting, no fixed survey schedule.
