How Credits Work

Simple, predictable usage metering

A credit is the unit of AI work consumed each time the assistant reads context, reasons, and produces an answer. We've made the math straightforward so your team can plan with confidence — no surprise bills, no opaque metering.

1 credit = 10,000 tokens (input + output combined)
