Causal Inference AI

Overview

Our Causal Inference AI moves beyond traditional correlation-based analytics to identify root causes of operational issues. By modeling complex cause-and-effect relationships across your mining operations, this solution pinpoints the fundamental drivers behind equipment failures, production bottlenecks, and quality variations. Unlike black-box AI approaches, our system provides transparent, explainable insights that enable data-driven decision making and targeted interventions.

AI Insights

Lubrication Quality shows the highest predictive lift (0.9) on Component Wear in our demo dataset – When viscosity dips below 88 cSt, the model highlights roughly a one-in-three failure risk within three weeks, illustrating how maintenance teams could schedule bearing swaps before thermal runaway.

Impact (illustrative): Converts reactive work into planned interventions, pointing to ~18% downtime avoidance once live data is connected.

Dust Contamination appears as the earliest environmental leading indicator in the sample data – A simulated 12% particulate spike precedes crusher faults by ~18 days, demonstrating how predictive cleaning cycles can replace emergency shutdowns.

Impact (illustrative): Suggests a 15-20% uplift in asset uptime while trimming surprise spend on filters and seals.
