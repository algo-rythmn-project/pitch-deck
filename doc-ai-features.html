The pitch

The same problem across every regulated industry

Organizations drown in documents they can't search intelligently, can't cross-reference against regulation, and can't update without expensive professional help. Document Intelligence replaces that bottleneck with a cited, always-current AI layer grounded in the client's own files.

The problem
Documents exist. Answers don't.
Every organization has the records. None have a way to ask them a plain-language question and get a cited answer in seconds.
The cost today
Lawyers and consultants at $500–$2,000/hr.
The knowledge work Document Intelligence replaces is expensive, slow, and lost when the professional moves on.
Our edge
Cited answers from the client's own files.
Not general AI. Every answer is grounded in the client's documents plus their specific regulatory corpus — with source citations.

Deployment model

The platform doesn't change. The corpus does.

Document Intelligence deploys identically across every industry. A pilot requires one document set and delivers cited answers within days.

One high-pain document set — 50 or more files in any format. Narrow, real, and measurable. No IT project required to start.
02
Prove the query layer
We ingest the corpus, load the regulatory rulebook, and answer the questions your team asks every day. Measure time saved against the manual baseline.
03
Scale the library
Once proven on one domain, the platform extends across the full compliance library. Each domain adds a corpus — not a rebuild.
