Advanced NHL Equivalency Projection

Our AI layer will enhance your existing NHLe models by incorporating contextual factors that simple multipliers miss:

  • Context-Aware Analytics: Weighing performance against league strength, teammate quality, and high-leverage situations (e.g., playoff performance).
  • Biomechanical Integration: Using skeletal pose estimation from video to analyze "explosive gait" and joint angles, predicting adaptability to NHL pace.
  • Micro-Action Forecasting: Predicting NHL success based on "invisible" habits like stick-blade positioning and pre-pass scanning frequency.
  • Dynamic Coefficients: Moving beyond static league multipliers to player-specific translation factors that evolve with performance trends.
