Intuitive Strategy Session

Based on synthesized insights, coaching staff holds meeting to develop intuitive strategy adjustments for next game.

5

Manual Player Communication

Coaches manually compile video clips and notes to share with individual players about adjustments needed.

AI-Powered Future Workflow

1

Automated Report Synthesis

AI Agent auto-generates narrative report highlighting key tactical failures/successes from integrated data streams immediately post-game.

2

Intelligent Video Processing

AI automatically tags key moments, generates highlight reels for coaching review, and syncs clips with relevant metrics.

Conversational Strategy Exploration

Coach asks: "Which defensive pairings had the lowest success rate against their 1-2-2 forecheck, and what were their most common failed exit types?" AI instantly analyzes and responds.

4

Predictive Simulation & Planning

AI runs "what-if" simulations for next game: "Simulate using more stretch passes against Team Coastal's forecheck based on their O-zone time%."

5

Automated Player Development

AI generates personalized video clips and training recommendations for each player based on their performance gaps, sent automatically.
